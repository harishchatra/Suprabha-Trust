Vedic Energy Sciences

Bio Field Sciences — The Living Energy Matrix

Modern science has extensively mapped the physical body through its Lymphatic, Nervous, Cardiovascular, and Visceral systems. Ancient Vedic knowledge, however, recognises that the human being extends far beyond these visible systems — into the realm of Human Energy Fields and the Kosha system of subtle sheaths.

"There is ONE divine supreme Universal force that guides us all. This loving, caring, nurturing strength and energy resides in every SOUL." — Dr Sujata Vaidya

Foundation — The Kosha System

The Human Body — Beyond the Physical

The Kosha system in Vedic tradition describes five sheaths of existence surrounding the Atman (soul). Bio-field science provides the modern instrumentation to observe and measure these ancient Vedic insights — the living energy matrix that underlies all physical existence.

"Each entity, each life, each iota of thought has this Divine supreme SOUL energy. We are ONE, and together we will rise." — Dr Sujata Vaidya

Five Koshas — Sheaths of Existence

1
Annamaya Kosha — Physical Body

The gross physical body nourished by food (Anna). The anatomical structure mapped by modern medicine — bones, muscles, organs, fluids. The foundation layer through which all other sheaths operate.

2
Pranamaya Kosha — Vital Energy Body

The body of Prana (life force) — the subtle energy layer that animates the physical body. Corresponds to the bio-field measurable through Kirlian photography and PIP imaging. Controlled through Pranayama (breathing science).

3
Manomaya Kosha — Mental Body

The sheath of mind, emotions, and instincts. Thoughts, beliefs, and emotional patterns reside here — and are directly reflected in the bio-field. Negative thoughts contract and deplete the aura; positive thoughts expand and brighten it.

4
Vijnanamaya Kosha — Wisdom Body

The sheath of intellect, discrimination, and higher understanding. As spiritual practice deepens, this sheath becomes more active, reflected in increasingly structured and luminous aura patterns visible in bio-field imaging.

5
Anandamaya Kosha — Bliss Body

The subtlest sheath — the body of pure bliss surrounding the Atman (soul). Accessible through deep meditation, samadhi, and surrender. Corresponds to the highest-frequency aura patterns documented in advanced meditators.

Kirlian Photography — Capturing the Bio-field

Kirlian Photography — Scientific Evidence

Kirlian photography is a technique that captures the electro-photonic emanations (aura/bio-field) of living and non-living objects on photographic film. It provides visible evidence of the energy fields that surround all matter — and has produced landmark findings that validate ancient Vedic understanding of life force.

The Phantom Leaf Experiment

Kirlian images of a leaf with a portion physically removed still show the complete leaf's energy field — demonstrating that the bio-field template precedes and outlasts physical form. This directly confirms the Vedic teaching that the Pranamaya Kosha (energy body) is the blueprint for the physical body.

Fresh vs Stored Food

Kirlian images clearly show the superior energy field of fresh food compared to stored food — direct, visual evidence of life force (Prana) depletion over time. The ancient Ayurvedic principle of eating fresh, seasonal, locally grown food receives scientific validation through bio-field imaging.

Wholemeal vs White Bread

Kirlian imaging shows measurably higher bio-field energy in wholemeal bread versus refined white bread — confirming the Ayurvedic preference for whole, unprocessed foods as higher-prana nutrition. Refining and processing depletes the life-force of food, visible and measurable in Kirlian photography.

Microwaves Deplete Energy

One of the most striking findings: Kirlian images of microwaved food versus fresh food show a dramatic depletion of bio-field energy. A fresh apple shows a rich, expansive energy field — the same apple after microwaving shows drastically reduced or absent energy. Modern convenience, ancient loss.

Herbs, Spices & Sacred Foods

Each herb and food item shows a unique, characteristic energy signature in Kirlian photography. Garlic, pepper, Tulsi (holy basil), and Neem each display distinct and powerful energy patterns — validating the classical Ayurvedic and Vedic classification of foods and plants as energy medicines.

Sacred Objects

Rudraksha beads show a significantly elevated, structured energy field in Kirlian photography — with measurably different patterns when ceremonially invoked. Divine writings, sacred texts, and Yantras all show energy fields far beyond those of ordinary objects — providing measurable evidence for their traditional sacred function.

PIP Technology — Polycontrast Interference Photography

Aura Mapping & Pre-Prognosis Assessment

PIP (Polycontrast Interference Photography) technology provides a visual, colour-coded record of the human bio-field. It enables pre-diagnostic assessment of disease, measurement of the effects of spiritual practice, and documentation of thought and emotion on the energy field.

Aura Changes with Thought & Emotion

  • Stressed with positive hyperactivity — expanded but chaotic, jagged aura patterns
  • Stressed and depressed person — contracted, dark, depleted aura with significant energy gaps
  • Anger — sharp, red, disrupted energy spikes around the aura
  • Love and Compassion — warm, expansive, coherent green and pink aura patterns
  • Depression — collapsed, dark, inward-pulling energy field

Change in thought patterns directly changes the AURA — providing a measurable basis for the Vedic teaching that thought is the most powerful force in shaping human reality and health.

Pre-Prognosis Disease Assessment

PIP imaging can reveal energy disruptions in specific chakra and organ regions before conventional medical tests detect physical pathology. A 42-year-old editor with no complaints showed stress on Heart Chakra and Throat Chakra — suggesting undiagnosed cardiac or thymus problems. Early energy-field assessment offers a non-invasive diagnostic tool.

Before & After Injury

Before injury: full, coherent, symmetrical aura patterns. After injury: disruption, depletion, and asymmetry in the affected energy zones. Ill health is reflected in the back-view aura image before physical symptoms are clinically detectable — suggesting the bio-field as an early-warning system for physical health.

After Prayer & Positive Thinking

  • Expanded, more coherent, and symmetrical aura after prayer
  • Brighter, higher-frequency colours after meditation
  • Stressed energy fields restored to healthy patterns after consistent practice
  • Higher consciousness states visually reflected as larger, more luminous auras

Energy Projection from Hands

PIP imaging of healing practitioners during energy work (Reiki, therapeutic touch, blessings) shows remarkable energy projection from the hands. Blessings given by spiritual masters show measurably higher energy projection than untrained individuals — validating the ancient tradition of healing touch.

Pran Pratishthana — Temple Consecration

PIP imaging of temples and sacred spaces before and after Pran Pratishthana (the ritual consecration of idols and sacred spaces) shows dramatic energetic transformation. Temple Shikhar (spires) photographed after Pran Pratishthan show significantly elevated and structured energy fields — consistent with their traditional role as energy transmitters and receivers of cosmic energy.

Food Energies & Pranayama

Food as Energy Medicine — Pranayama Science

PIP research shows that the effect of food on the bio-field is rapid and dramatic — within 5 minutes of consuming food, measurable changes in the aura are visible. This validates the Ayurvedic principle that food selection is a matter of energy medicine, not merely nutrition.

PIP Food Energy Research

  • Palak (Spinach) — high bio-field energy; rich prana content
  • Green Chilli — intense, concentrated energy signature
  • Wheat (Gahu) — balanced, stable energy field
  • Nuts — high-density energy content; strong bio-field
  • Supari + Tobacco — disrupted, chaotic energy signature — consistent with harmful physiological effects

These findings validate the ancient Ayurvedic classification of foods as Sattvic (pure), Rajasic (stimulating), and Tamasic (depleting).

Effect of Pranayama on the Aura

Pranayama (Vedic breathing science) produces measurable, rapid changes in the human bio-field. Before-and-after PIP imaging of pranayama sessions shows significant expansion, brightening, and stabilisation of the aura — consistent with ancient descriptions of pranayama as a method for increasing and purifying prana (life force).

  • Immediate expansion of the aura visible in PIP imaging
  • Increased brightness and coherence of energy field colours
  • Significant reduction in chaotic, disruptive aura patterns
  • Sustained effects measured up to 6 hours after practice

Agnihotra — Effect on Environment

Agnihotra (the ancient Vedic fire ritual performed at sunrise and sunset) has been studied using PIP imaging of the surrounding environment. Before-and-after images show significant energetic transformation — enhanced brightness, coherence, and expansion of the environmental energy field. This provides scientific evidence for the ancient claim that Homa/Yagna rituals purify and energise the surrounding ecology.

Objects Around Us — Impact on Energy

All electronic chips contain Quartz chips. Jewellery, wrist watches, mobiles, and PCs contain high-energy Quartz chips. These energies often do not match personal energy and cause unexplainable pain and swelling — a bio-field incompatibility measurable through Kirlian and PIP imaging. Ancient Vedic guidelines on metals, gemstones, and materials worn on the body reflect this understanding.

Spiritual Sciences — Yantra, Tantra & Mantra

Spiritual Energy Transfers & Sacred Sciences

Divine knowledge and energies are transferred to humankind through three primary instruments. Bio-field science provides measurable evidence for what Vedic tradition has described for millennia — the living energy reality of prayer, mantra, meditation, and sacred geometry.

Yantra — Geometric Energy Tools

Yantras are geometric instruments that channel and focus energy. PIP imaging of the Shree Yantra — one of the most powerful geometric instruments in Vedic tradition — shows extraordinary energy concentration and projection. The sacred geometry of the Yantra acts as a lens that focuses and amplifies subtle energy.

Tantra — Disciplined Energy Methods

Tantra describes disciplined methods for working with subtle energy. PIP and Kirlian imaging of Tantric practitioners and ritual spaces provides measurable evidence that the directed application of intention, breath, and body position produces significant and verifiable changes in the bio-field.

Mantra — Vibrational Sound Science

Mantras are vibrational syllables that resonate with specific cosmic frequencies. Recitation of mantras produces measurable expansion and brightening of the aura in PIP imaging. The Rig Veda and sacred texts photographed in Kirlian show energy fields far beyond ordinary books — living energy systems, not merely information.

Meditation — Building Higher Consciousness

  • Regular meditation builds inner strength — visible as progressively larger, more coherent auras
  • Random, non-systematic thoughts cause confusion and pain — visible as incoherent aura patterns
  • A calm mind after meditation shows strength in each thought — leading to meaningful outcomes
  • Higher thinking and meditation resolve daily stress — visibly bringing inner peace and brighter aura
