About Us
Our story, mission, and the three dimensions of Sanatana Dharma practice
2 / 15

Suprabha Trust has worked since 2006 to discover and document the mystic cultures, sciences, and rituals performed across many parts of India — honoring a civilization whose depth and diversity gave rise to Hinduism, Sikhism, Buddhism, and beyond.

Suprabha Trust

Dedicated to preserving and promoting India's rich traditional and cultural sciences through conservation, education, and community engagement.
