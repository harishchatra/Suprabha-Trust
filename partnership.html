Collaborate With Us

Associations & Partnerships

We collaborate with educational institutions, research organizations, cultural bodies, and community groups to preserve India's heritage together.

Academic

Academic Institutions

Universities, research centers, and educational institutions working on traditional knowledge systems. Collaborate on research projects and knowledge exchange.

Cultural

Cultural Organizations

Museums, cultural centers, and heritage foundations preserving traditional arts and practices. Partner on exhibitions and documentation projects.

Community

Community Groups

Local communities, artisan collectives, and traditional practitioners. Grassroots initiatives supporting traditional knowledge holders within communities.

Corporate

Corporate Partners

Businesses committed to cultural preservation through CSR initiatives. Develop meaningful partnerships creating lasting impact on heritage conservation.

1

Initial Contact

Reach out with information about your organization and areas of interest. We'll schedule a meeting to discuss collaboration opportunities.

2

Identify Common Goals

Together we'll identify shared objectives and explore how our organizations complement each other's strengths and resources.

3

Partnership Framework

We'll create a clear framework outlining roles, responsibilities, timelines, and expected outcomes for collaborative initiatives.

4

Implementation

Launch collaborative projects with regular monitoring and evaluation to ensure meaningful impact on heritage preservation.
