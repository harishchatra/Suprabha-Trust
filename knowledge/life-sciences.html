Life Sciences — 18 Models of Human & Natural Systems

Suprabha Trust Research Team  ·  Life Sciences Series  ·  Hyderabad

Humanistic sciences work to understand the workings of the Human Body with nature, society, and everyday activities — embedded in frequencies expressed through classical art forms, engineering, and sciences like Vaasthu and Astrology.

Model 1 — Humanistic Sciences

Human existence is influenced by vibrations and elements: water, food, posture, light, stone, color, organic fabrics, medicine, oils, aromas, flora and fauna, herbs, cereals, and cosmic energy. These influences operate through energies embedded in specific frequencies. It is essential for the human frequency to stay close to the frequency of the Earth. The five fingers act as energy points connecting to the Pancha Bhoothas — Agni, Vaayu, Jal, Prithvi, and Akaash.

  • Human Geometry — 2 hands, eyes, legs, ears; 1 nose, mouth; 5 fingers, toes
  • Activating and withdrawal of five senses at children's level
  • Natural instincts: breathing, crying, eating
  • Intelligence towards society and nature
  • Basic activities: consuming food, intake of air, hearing, seeing, talking, sensing

Model 4 — Skeletal & Biological Systems

  • Muscle Mass — lean tissue, reduces with aging; maintaining it becomes more important as adults grow older
  • Bone Marrow — produces Red Blood cells (Oxygen), White Blood cells (Immunity), Platelets (Clotting)
  • Blood — Plasma 55%, RBC/WBC 45%; 7–8% of total body weight
  • Plasma — Water 92%, Proteins 7%; carries nutrients, antibodies, hormones; removes waste
  • Fat — subcutaneous & visceral; long-term energy storage, insulation, hormone production

Model 5 — Five Vital Organs

  • Brain — body's control center; processing information, thoughts, and movements
  • Heart — pumps oxygenated blood and nutrients to all cells; removes waste
  • Lungs — exchange oxygen and carbon dioxide; allowing breathing
  • Liver — detoxifies blood, processes nutrients, stores energy
  • Kidneys — filter waste from blood to produce urine; maintain fluid balance

  • Mechanical — kinetic energy (movement) + potential energy (stored)
  • Electronics — ions like charges, nerves like wires, ATP the fuel
  • Frequency Machine — electrical currents for pain relief, muscle stimulation, wellness

Model 10 — Nervous System & Organ Frequencies

The length of the human nervous system equals the diameter of the Earth (~8,000 miles). The heart — the size of a small fist — pumps blood across 60,000 miles, covering Earth's diameter 108 times. The heart beats 70–75 times per minute and is considered the second brain due to its intuitive abilities.

  • Heart — 67 Hz
  • Lungs — 220 Hz
  • Kidneys — 390 Hz
  • Liver — 55 to 60 Hz
  • Brain — Gamma, Alpha, Beta, Theta, Delta waves

Model 2 — Ayurveda

Ayurveda provides insights into all aspects of plants, herbs, metals, and astrophysics. It focuses on Tridoshas — Vaatha, Pittha, and Kapha — sensed through three Naadi channels: Ida (Chandra Naadi, golden-white, Beejaksha: OM), Pingala (Surya Naadi, white-silver, Beejaksha: Ham), and Sushumna (Central Naadi, red-orange, Beejaksha: Ram).
