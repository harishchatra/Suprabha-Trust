Submission Guidelines

1

Content must be relevant

Videos should focus on traditional and cultural sciences of India — rituals, heritage sites, philosophy, arts, or related subjects.

2

Original or rights-cleared content only

Submit only videos you filmed yourself or have explicit permission to share and publish.

3

Accepted formats & platforms

YouTube, Vimeo, Google Drive, or Dropbox links accepted. Minimum 480p resolution. Any duration welcome.

4

Review process

All submissions are reviewed by our editorial team before publication. You will be notified by email within 7“10 working days.

5

Credit & attribution

Your name and any organisation you represent will be credited alongside the published video.
