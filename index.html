Our Work

Impact Areas

From sacred architecture to ancient manuscripts, our work spans the full breadth of India's living heritage.

Temple Architecture & Sacred Spaces

Restoration and documentation of ancient temples, sacred groves, and architectural marvels that embody India's spiritual and artistic heritage across centuries.

Traditional Arts & Crafts

Supporting artisans and craftspeople in preserving traditional techniques in textiles, metalwork, pottery, painting, and other heritage crafts at risk of disappearing.

Ancient Manuscripts & Texts

Digitization, translation, and preservation of ancient manuscripts containing knowledge on medicine, astronomy, philosophy, and cultural practices of India.

Ritual Sciences & Practices

Documentation and study of traditional rituals, ceremonies, and practices that form the living, breathing heritage of Indian communities across regions.
